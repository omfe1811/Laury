About the Exhibition

Welcome to "Love’s Labour," an interactive artistic journey showcasing a curated selection of mixed media artworks and prose poems by Laury Garcia.

This thought-provoking collection delves into the complexities of love, personal growth, and identity. As you explore the exhibition, we invite you to discover the vibrant stories that connect us.

We encourage you to reflect on your own perspectives about love and identity, because visitors are invited to contribute to the exhibition if they wish. Join us in celebrating the diverse narratives that shape our understanding of these profound themes.

Laury Who Dat?

Laury is a Latina queer artist that went from Miami vibes to Oslo chills. She’s here to tackle love, growth, and identity— no pressure, right? ...because who doesn’t love a good existentialist?

Come celebrate the wild and wonderful narratives that make life so delightfully complicated!

Let’s unravel these quirky narratives together. We promise, no judgment!
