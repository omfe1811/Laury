About the Exhibition

Welcome to "Love’s Labour," an interactive artistic journey showcasing a curated selection of mixed media artworks and prose poems by Laury Garcia. This thought-provoking collection delves into the complexities of love, personal growth, and identity. As you explore the exhibition, we invite you to discover the vibrant stories that connect us. We encourage you to reflect on your own perspectives about love and identity, because visitors are invited to contribute to the exhibition if they wish. Join us in celebrating the diverse narratives that shape our understanding of these profound themes.
